STABILITY

STABILITY is a sophisticated personal financial management application designed to bridge the gap between daily spending and long-term financial security. The projects core innovation lies in its intelligent "Safe to Spend" engine, which meticulously calculates a users discretionary income by analyzing fixed obligations, recurring debts, and personal savings targets against their total earnings. I, along with my talented collaborators Kamrul and Tajemul, successfully developed the complete Flutter UI, transforming a complex financial logic into a seamless, high-performance mobile experience. The user journey begins with a secure authentication flow featuring Google integration and OTP verification, leading into a comprehensive onboarding process that captures critical data regarding income stability and pay frequency. We focused on building a highly responsive interface that manages detailed inputs for rent, mortgages, and utility bills, ultimately generating a dynamic "Stability Profile." Our technical implementation ensures that as users set aspirational goals—such as purchasing high-end electronics or establishing emergency funds—the UI reflects real-time recalculations and progress tracking. By adhering to a clean, minimalist design philosophy, we ensured that every functional component, from debt management to subscription handling, remains intuitive and visually cohesive, providing users with absolute financial clarity in a premium digital environment.

CoachMe Hub

CoachMe is a comprehensive Flutter-based mobile application designed to streamline interactions between athletes and coaches. The app supports two distinct roles athletes and coaches each with tailored features to enhance engagement and training. Athletes can book sessions with coaches, make secure payments, chat with coaches after payment, and access premium coaching videos and session updates. Coaches, after subscribing to a premium plan, can manage their booked athletes, view upcoming sessions through a dynamic dashboard, update sessions, and share premium content. The application also includes a marketplace where athletes and coaches can buy and sell services or products, with integrated cart, checkout, and secure payment functionality for seamless transactions. Developed collaboratively with Kamrul Hasan, Tajemul Islam, Shraboni Akter, and Ariful Islam, we implemented robust API integration, real-time chat, and efficient payment flows. CoachMe demonstrates the ability to deliver scalable, user-centric, and high-performance mobile applications, combining complex business logic with an intuitive and visually engaging Flutter UI.

Community Support

Developed a Flutter application designed to connect help-seekers with volunteers during emergencies and natural disasters. The platform functions as a specialized social ecosystem where users can post assistance requests, while volunteers provide real-time support. Help-seekers can broadcast urgent needs, track aid, and manage their profiles, while volunteers can browse nearby requests, manage their contribution history, and engage through integrated chat and video calling for seamless coordination. The application also features a social engagement engine—allowing users to like, comment, and share community updates—alongside critical modules like an SOS Panic Button for immediate location sharing and a Live Crisis Map to visualize high-need areas. As the sole developer for the Flutter UI implementation, I focused on creating a responsive and high-performance interface that remains intuitive under high-stress scenarios. This project demonstrates my ability to build scalable, user-centric mobile solutions by combining complex social features with essential emergency response functionality.

Menu Assists

Menu Assists is an innovative Flutter-based mobile application designed to help users make healthier and more informed dining choices when visiting restaurants.
