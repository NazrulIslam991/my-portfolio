Menu Assists is a collaborative Flutter app that simplifies healthy dining through smart menu scanning. It provides personalized dish recommendations based on users' dietary goals, featuring a health dashboard, nutritional tracking, and real-time restaurant discovery. By merging intuitive UI with data-driven insights, it empowers users to maintain a healthy lifestyle while eating out.
